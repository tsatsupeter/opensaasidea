## After Approval

Once you receive affiliate links, update them in:
**`/src/lib/affiliates.ts`**

Replace the plain URLs with your tracked affiliate URLs. For example:
```ts
'vercel': { url: 'https://vercel.com/?ref=openprojectidea', tag: 'Deploy on Vercel' },
'supabase': { url: 'https://supabase.com/?ref=openprojectidea', tag: 'Start with Supabase' },
```

Also update the share menu links in:
**`/src/components/ideas/share-menu.tsx`**

For Lovable and Replit, replace the base URLs with your referral URLs.
